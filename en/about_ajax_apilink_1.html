Profil
Digital minded branding consultancy

Beaubourg was born in 2014 from the association of Mickael Tsakiris and Sylvain Capeyron. In a rapidly changing market of digital studios and agencies, the atypical nature of this duo (strategy + design) has enabled it from the outset to envisage projects from a new creative angle: starting with the brand and bringing out its truth through design, considering digital touchpoints a high-priority. The arrival of Rémy Blamaud (formerly Octave & Octave) as Lead Developer has enabled Beaubourg to take the vision initiated by Sylvain and Mickael a step further, by adding the technical scope of the projects. 

Today, we are a team of 5 articulated around 2 partners with complementary profiles and experiences validated in agencies and within brands. A growing consultancy that teams up with brands and takes charge of their projects as a whole, without having to outsource a key skill or know-how. This approach allows us to guarantee a high level of quality to the deliverables we produce for our customers.

Beaubourg claims a radical, demanding and free spirit. 

Team
Mickael Tsakiris
Partner — Strategy
Gratuated in 2005 with a Bachelor of Arts (HONS), Mickaël worked at Saint Laurent, Bon Marché group (LVMH) and managed luxury accounts within agencies such as Mazarine and Australie. He advised brands such as Chanel, Moët & Chandon, Belvedère, Carita and Guerlain. In love with premium brands and their image stakes, he founds Beaubourg with Sylvain in 2015. He is in charge of strategy, customer relations and new business. Mickael is fond of industrial design, Bauhaus architecture, greek food and boxing.
Rémy Blamaud
Lead Developer
Remy graduated in 2010 with a BTS in Computer Science in Management & Development, and began his career at Wyniwyg agency, then in the digital studio The Twelve. After a period of startup, he joined Octave & Octave in 2015 where he worked on international projects, notably for Guerlain. Remy joined Beaubourg in January 2019, where he holds the position of Lead Developer. In this capacity, he is in charge of web development at large and contributes to the elaboration of technical solutions for all projects. Rémy is also responsible for the technological survey and innovation strategy of the office.
Masis Gülmez
Web developer
After spending almost 10 years in the banking industry, Masis decided to change jobs and returned to school. He has chosen to integrate HETIC into the "Expert in Information and Communication Technologies" course. Driven by the need to always keep learning, he likes to challenge himself and take up new challenges. He also loves to share what he learns, and works as a freelancer in a training centre to support those who choose to convert to web jobs. Passionate about music, he (almost) always has a headset on his ears, but his eyes are wide open on the web!
Benjamin Chareyron
Web Developer
With over three years of experience in web development for personal, freelance and entrepreneurial projects, Benjamin is a 23 years old developer pursuing his 5th year at HETIC within the "Information and Communication Technologies Expert" program. He joined the Beaubourg Tech team in 2018 to strengthen the agency's fast development. Driven by an irresistible thirst for learning and a search for excellence, he continues every day to sharpen his skills, develops entrepreneurial projects, loves to snack almonds and does his best to make the web more inspiring.
